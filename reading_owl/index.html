What and Why?

The purpose of the program is to provide users with an intelligent and interactive chat-based information assistant (not chat-based yet). Leveraging GPT models, the program aims to enhance the user's ability to retrieve accurate and relevant information across a wide range of topics. The project originated from a desire to try out LLM APIs to build something I'd personally like to use. And with rampant misinformation around us all, Reading Owl seemed like a useful tool.

Key Features and Goals

1. Diverse Knowledge Base: The program taps into extensive knowledge repositories, enabling users to ask questions on various subjects and receive informative responses.

2.Lateral Reading Assistance: Designed to aid users in lateral reading tasks, the assistant generates insightful questions that encourage users to explore diverse perspectives and sources.

3. Summarization Capabilities: The program can summarize information from multiple sources, providing concise and coherent answers to user queries.

How It Benefits Users:

Efficient Information Retrieval: Users can quickly obtain information without the need for extensive searches, streamlining the research process.

Promotes Critical Thinking: By generating insightful questions, the program promotes critical thinking and encourages users to consider multiple viewpoints.

Lateral Reading Support: Facilitates lateral reading tasks by summarizing relevant information and providing questions that prompt users to explore further.

User-Friendly Interface: The chat-based interaction makes the program accessible to a broad audience, irrespective of technical expertise.

Time-Saving: Users can save time by receiving concise summaries and relevant questions, avoiding the need to sift through lengthy documents.
